🌱 What I'm Building
🌲 Chianya — Forest of Consciousness

"You don't have to explain anything. Just arrive as you are."

A live AI-powered emotional wellbeing platform for youth aged 16–28. Not a therapy app. A 3D forest sanctuary that responds to how you feel — with an AI companion, 6 therapeutic modes, crisis detection routed to real helplines, and a Letter to Future Self feature that delivers your words back to you 30 days later.
Live: https://chianya.vercel.app
Stack: React.js · Three.js · Node.js · Express · MongoDB Atlas · Groq AI · Framer Motion · PWA
SDGs: UN SDG 3 · UN SDG 4

🎓 KAIROS — For the First-Generation Girl
An AI-powered scholarship and guidance platform for first-generation college girls from Tier 2/3 Indian cities. AI mentor KAI helps them find scholarships, navigate careers, and know they are not alone.
Status: In development — deploying soon
Stack: React.js · Node.js · MongoDB · Groq AI
SDGs: UN SDG 4 · UN SDG 5
